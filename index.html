The person you see above, wandering some hills in India, goes by the name of Ishkaran Singh.
He is known for being a game programmer but these days he is busy making stuff for gamers and geeks.
Apart from that rumours are that he loves gaming, dancing, reading and bike rides among other things. He previously worked at Ubisoft on FarCry Classic and Just Dance franchise, after which he moved to IIT Bombay and worked in Unity at another gaming project before leaving everything and moving to bangalore to start his own gaming/geek merchandise brand, Psycho Store.
